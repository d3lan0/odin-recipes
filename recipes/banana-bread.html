Banana Bread Recipe

Description

Delicious and simple banana bread recipe that a wonderful redditor posted. It's vegan and it made it to the front page.


Ingredients

  • 3 Large Bananas
  • 1/3 to 1/4 Cups brown sugar
  • 1/4 Cup canola oil
  • 1/4 Cup almond milk
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • a pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Heat oven to 350degrees fahrenheit

    2. In a bowl...

  2. Chop up three really large-ish over ripe bananas and throw them in a mixing bowl.
  3. Add sugar and mash
  4. Add Canola Oil, milk, vanilla, cinnamon and nutmeg and mix
  5. Sieve flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt over wet mixture
  6. Fold in the dry ingredientsDO NOT MIX
  7. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan
  8. Baked for 30-40 mins. When its done an inserted toothpick should come out clean.

    9. ENJOY!
