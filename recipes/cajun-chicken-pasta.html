Cajun Chicken Pasta

Description

This kicked-up cajun chicken pasta recipe is a family favorite and my most requested dish. It's easy to adapt, too. Swap in shrimp for the chicken, add your favorite veggies, adjust the spice level to your family's taste—you can't go wrong! —Dolly Kragel, Smithland, Iowa


Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 2x1/2-in. strips
  • 3 teaspoons Cajun seasoning
  • 8 ounces uncooked penne pasta (about 2-1/3 cups)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 1 small sweet red pepper, diced
  • 1 small green pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon lemon-pepper seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Pepper to taste
  • Chopped plum tomatoes
  • Minced fresh basil
  • Shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Toss chicken with Cajun seasoning; let stand 15 minutes. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.
  2. Toss chicken with Cajun seasoning; let stand 15 minutes. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.
  3. In same pan, heat remaining butter over medium-high heat; saute peppers, mushrooms and green onions until peppers are crisp-tender, 6-8 minutes
  4. Stir in cream and seasonings; bring to a boil. Cook and stir until slightly thickened, 4-6 minutes
  5. Stir in pasta and chicken; heat through.
  6. Top with tomatoes and basil.
  7. Sprinkle with cheese.li>
